Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Ghee
  • Flour

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk to form a soft dough and shape into small balls.
  2. Boil sugar and water until dissolved, then add cardamom powder, rose water, and saffron. Simmer briefly and keep warm.
  3. Heat ghee or oil on medium-low heat, fry the dough balls until golden brown, and drain on paper towels.
  4. Place the fried balls in the warm sugar syrup and let them soak for at least 2 hours.
  5. Serve the Gulab Jamun warm or at room temperature, optionally garnished with chopped nuts.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Granulated Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Ghee
  • Dry Fruits and Silver leaf
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Combine 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water in a pan. Heat until the sugar dissolves and the mixture reaches a one-string consistency.
  2. Reduce heat and gradually stir in 2 cups milk powder. Add 1/4 cup ghee and mix until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Stir in 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder and cook for a few more minutes until smooth and thick.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased tray, flatten the top, and garnish with chopped nuts. Optionally, decorate with silver leaf.
  5. Let the barfi cool completely, then cut into desired shapes and serve.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Ghee
  • Silver leaf
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Blend 2 cups cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  3. Add cashew powder to the syrup and cook until it thickens. Stir in 1 tablespoon ghee and 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder.
  4. Knead the mixture into a dough, then roll it out to 1/4-inch thickness.
  5. Let it cool slightly, then cut into diamond shapes and optionally decorate with silver leaf.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Dry Fruits
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Khoya

Recipe

  1. Grate 1 kg carrots. Sauté in 4 tbsp ghee for 10 minutes.
  2. Add 1 liter milk. Cook until milk evaporates (45 minutes to 1 hour).
  3. Stir in 1 cup sugar. Cook until thickened.
  4. Add 1/2 tsp cardamom powder and 200g crumbled khoya (optional).
  5. Fry 10-12 chopped cashews, 10-12 chopped almonds, and 10-12 raisins in ghee. Mix in and serve hot.
